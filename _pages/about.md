Hi there! 👋 I am Di Wu (吴迪), currently a Ph.D. student in the SC Group at the [Research Center for Social Computing and Information Retrieval (SCIR)](http://ir.hit.edu.cn/), **Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT)**. 

My research is primarily driven by the goal of making advanced AI systems more capable, reliable, and secure. I am particularly passionate about the alignment, safety, and continuous improvement of large-scale models.

### 🔬 Research Interests
My current research focuses on:
* **LLM Post-training:** Exploring efficient and effective methodologies to align and enhance Large Language Models after their initial pre-training phase.
* **AI Security & Safety:** Investigating the robust security mechanisms and vulnerability assessments for Large Language Models (LLMs), Vision-Language Models (VLMs), and Autonomous Agents.
